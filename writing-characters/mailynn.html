Mailynn Thomas

Role in Book

Mailynn is the main character of the book and is the narator. The book will be written in her point of view.

Her best friend's name is Jessica. She has a boyfriend named Daniel. Her friends sometimes call her Mai and her family sometimes calls her Lynn.

Physical Description

Mailynn is 5'10", has blonde hair, brown eyes and likes to dress very casual most of the time. The only times that she will "dress up" is when going out on a date or for a job interview. She is the older sister of two twin brothers, Colton and Martin Thomas.
